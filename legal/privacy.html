Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2024

Kaminski Performance ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Coin mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Summary: We respect your privacy. The Coin App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Coin App is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

2. Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored ONLY on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Flip History: Records of your coin flips (heads/tails results, timestamps)
  • App Preferences: Your settings (dark mode, sounds, animations)
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have purchased Coin Pro

This data is stored using secure on-device storage and can be deleted at any time through the app settings.

3. In-App Purchases

Coin App offers optional in-app purchases (Coin Pro subscription). All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

Apple's privacy policy applies to all purchase transactions: Apple Privacy Policy

4. Analytics

We may use anonymous, aggregated analytics provided by Apple (App Analytics) to understand general usage patterns such as:

  • Number of app downloads
  • App crashes and performance issues
  • General device types and iOS versions

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions. RevenueCat receives anonymous subscription data. See their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store: For processing payments and managing subscriptions

6. Children's Privacy

The Coin App is suitable for all ages (4+). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, children can safely use the app.

7. Data Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit your data to external servers. Your data security depends on your device's security settings (passcode, Face ID, etc.).

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View your flip history in the Stats tab
  • Delete: Clear all history in Settings > Clear History
  • Uninstall: Removing the app deletes all local data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
